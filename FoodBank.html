Food Bank Referrals


  • In order to get help from the food bank you will need to be referred with a food voucher.
  • Food banks work with a number of local agencies such as, Citizen Advice, children centres and health centres.
  • When you visit a referral agency they will take some basic details from you to complete the voucher.
  • Once you have received a voucher, you can exchange this for a minimum of 3 days of emergency food at your nearest food bank centre.
